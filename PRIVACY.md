# Privacy Policy — Clipframe

**Last updated:** March 20, 2026

## Data Collection

Clipframe does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

## What Clipframe Does

- Captures screenshots of the currently visible tab when you click the extension
- Saves the image file directly to your local downloads folder
- Stores your preferences (image format and quality) locally using Chrome's built-in storage sync

## Permissions

- **activeTab** — Used only to capture the visible tab when you click the extension icon
- **downloads** — Used only to save the screenshot to your downloads folder
- **offscreen** — Used only to stitch full-page screenshot segments into a single image
- **storage** — Used only to save your format and quality preferences

## Third Parties

Clipframe does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. All processing happens locally in your browser.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/brandonpaldom/clipframe/issues](https://github.com/brandonpaldom/clipframe/issues).
